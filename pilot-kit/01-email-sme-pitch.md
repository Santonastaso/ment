# Email 1 — SME owner pitch (cold outreach)

**When to send:** First contact with an SME owner, founder, or HR lead. Cold or warm intro.

**Goal:** Get a 20-minute discovery call.

**Placeholders to fill:** `{NAME}`, `{COMPANY}`, `{CALENDAR_LINK}`, `{YOUR_NAME}`

---

**Subject line (pick one):**
- Peer mentoring for your team — without the corporate budget
- A 15-minute idea for {COMPANY}'s team development
- {COMPANY} + a peer-mentoring pilot — worth a call?

---

**Body:**

Hi {NAME},

Quick note — I'm running a small pilot I think might be interesting for {COMPANY}.

It's called MENT. The premise: people on a 15-person team often don't have the senior peers internally to learn from — there's just no critical mass. So we match them with peers at *other* small companies (always non-competing). A senior marketer at one SaaS gets paired with a senior marketer at a fintech who's solving a similar problem from a different angle.

For the company, this is professional development that doesn't compete with internal hiring. Your engineer gets sharper, but they're not getting poached — they're learning from someone in a totally different sector.

The first three pilots are free of charge in exchange for honest feedback on what works and what doesn't. The setup takes about 15 minutes from your side: a quick discovery call, then I send a 5-minute form your team fills in, and I handle matching, intros, and follow-ups.

Worth a 20-minute call to see if it'd fit?

{CALENDAR_LINK}

— {YOUR_NAME}
