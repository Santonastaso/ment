**Audience:** SME owners, founders, HR leads of companies with 5-50 employees.

**Goal:** Get them to book a 20-minute discovery call.

**Tone:** Pragmatic, not startup-glossy. Specific about what it is and who it's for. Short copy — Carrd reads better with white space.

Suggested page structure: hero → what it is → who it's for → how it works → comparison → privacy → pricing → final CTA. Each section is one short paragraph plus a bullet list.

## What is it?

A curated peer-mentoring program for employees at small and mid-sized companies. Every match is hand-picked by a human — not an algorithm — based on what each person wants to learn and what their match has actually shipped.

Sessions are 30 minutes, video, asynchronous if needed, anywhere in the world.

---

## Who is it for?

- Companies of **5 to 50 people** without the budget for individual coaching.
- Founders and HR leads who want to invest in their people without growing the L&D function.
- Any employee whose career moves faster when they get an outside perspective.

---

## How it works

1. **Setup (15 min on you).** A brief discovery call, then you forward a 5-minute form to your team.
2. **Onboarding (5 min per employee).** They share their skills, goals, and consent to be matched.
3. **Matching (1-2 weeks on us).** We find peers at other non-competing small companies and send anonymized suggestions to both sides.
4. **Sessions (30 min each).** When both say yes, we share names and a calendar link.
5. **Reflection (2 min on each side).** A short form after each session feeds future matches.

---

---

## Privacy by design

- Matches are **suggested anonymously**. Names and companies are revealed only after both sides agree to meet.
- Reflections are **private to the person who wrote them**. Companies never see what employees wrote.
- We never match employees with people at **named competitors** — you tell us who they are at signup, and we block.
