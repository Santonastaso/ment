## Frequently asked questions

You've been invited to MENT, a peer-mentoring program connecting people at small companies who can help each other grow. Here's what people usually ask before signing up.

---

**Will my company be told I'm matched with someone, or what I discussed?**

No. Your participation is between you and us. We never tell {COMPANY_NAME} who you've been matched with or what was talked about. The only thing they see is anonymous, aggregated data — for example, "12 of your 18 employees have completed at least one session."

---

**Will my full name be shared with strangers?**

Not at first. When we propose a match, the other person sees only your function, city, country, company size, industry, and the skills you can teach or want to learn. **Your full name and your company name are revealed only after both you and the other person say yes to meeting.**

---

**Will my manager know if I decline a match?**

No. We don't share match-acceptance data with anyone at your company. Decline freely.

---

**What happens with my post-session reflection?**

It's private to you. The other person never sees it. We use it (combined with everyone else's) to improve future matches, but nobody at your company ever reads it.

---

**Can I pick which industry or function I'm matched with?**

Indirectly. You set your own skills and learning goals on the onboarding form, and we match based on those. You can also choose at signup whether you're open to cross-industry matches — most people say yes.

---

**What if I'm matched with a competitor of {COMPANY_NAME}?**

We block this automatically. Each company we onboard tells us their named competitors, and we never propose matches across them.

---

**How much time will this take from me?**

- 5 minutes once, to fill the onboarding form.
- 30 minutes per session, typically once every 4-8 weeks. (You're never required to accept a match.)
- 2 minutes after each session for the reflection form.

---

**Do I get paid to mentor others?**

No. This is a peer-to-peer network — you give time, you also get time. Most people we've spoken to like that it's reciprocal: you're a mentor on something you've shipped, and a mentee on something you want to learn next. Sessions tend to flow both ways anyway.

---

**Can I quit at any time?**

Yes. Reply to any email from us with "please pause my account" or "please remove me" and we'll do it within a day. If you want to come back later, just send another email.

---

**Where is my data stored?**

On Airtable, hosted in the EU. We can provide more details if you need them for compliance — just ask {YOUR_EMAIL}.

---
