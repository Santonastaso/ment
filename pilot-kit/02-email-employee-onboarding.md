# Email 2 — Employee onboarding

**When to send:** Right after the SME admin has confirmed their team's participation. Either the SME admin sends this themselves, or you send it with the admin's blessing (cc'd).

**Goal:** Get the employee to fill the onboarding form.

**Placeholders:** `{EMPLOYEE_FIRST_NAME}`, `{COMPANY_NAME}`, `{ONBOARDING_FORM_LINK}` (your Airtable form URL: `https://airtable.com/appvnsJ96xqpW6YPa/shrLM3uctVf5BaI71`), `{YOUR_NAME}`

---

**Subject line:**
- A short form to get you started on MENT

---

**Body:**

Hi {EMPLOYEE_FIRST_NAME},

{COMPANY_NAME} has joined a small pilot of MENT — a peer-mentoring network for SME teams. Over the next few weeks I'll be matching you with people at other small companies who can either help you with something you're trying to learn, or who'd benefit from your experience.

To make matches that are actually useful, I need a quick five-minute form from you:

{ONBOARDING_FORM_LINK}

A few things to know upfront:

- When I suggest a match, the other person sees only your function, city, and the skills you can teach — not your name or your company. Your full name is only revealed after you both agree to meet.
- You can decline any match without explanation.
- Your reflections after a session are private to you. Nobody at {COMPANY_NAME} sees them — not your manager, not HR.

Reply to this email if you have any questions, or just fill in the form and you'll hear from me within a week with your first match.

— {YOUR_NAME}
