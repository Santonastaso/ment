# MENT — 12-week validation plan

**Thesis.** Curated peer-mentoring is a paid product for both SMEs and enterprises — they want different deliveries of it. We're validating both before building either.

## Two markets, one shape of problem

| | **SME (cross-company)** | **Enterprise (internal)** |
|---|---|---|
| Buyer | Founder / HR lead, 5-50 ppl | Head of People, 400+ ppl |
| MVP | Manual: Airtable + email | Existing software (dev MVP) |
| Price hypothesis | €5/seat/mo | €1k+/mo |

## Validation tracks

| Track | Window | Pass | Fail |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1a** SME pilot — 1-2 SMEs, free | Wks 1-5 | 1/2 commit to a paid follow-on | 0/2 → close |
| **1b** Enterprise demos — 8-10 HR/L&D leads | Wks 5-12 | 2/8 ask for a paid pilot | <1/8 → close |
| **1.5** Investor warmup | Wks 10-14 | 2+ "come back when X" | — |
| **2** Build the validated product | Wk 14+ | — | — |

## What we already have

- A working **internal-mentoring web app** — our enterprise demo asset on day one, not a slide.
- A **manual SME pilot kit** — Airtable + forms + email templates, ready to go.
- A **19-item V1 follow-ups doc** — scope honestly accounted for.

## Asks

- **Co-founder** — full-stack technical, ~6-10 weeks to adapt the MVP for cross-company. Customer-curious, ideally HR-tech / L&D / B2B-SaaS background.
- **Advisor** — 1 hour/month. Bonus if you've sold into SME-HR or enterprise-L&D budgets.
- **Introductions** — to SMEs (5-50 ppl) for the pilot, and HR/L&D leads at 400+-person companies for the demo track.

**Disclosed risk.** Founder works full-time; all MENT activity is unpaid until employer written permission is in place.
