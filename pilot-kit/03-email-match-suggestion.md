# Email 3 — Anonymized match suggestion

**When to send:** When you've identified a potential match. Send the SAME structure to BOTH employees in parallel (each describing the OTHER side anonymously).

**Goal:** Get a yes/no/not-now reply.

**Important:** Do NOT include any identifying info about the other person — no names, no company names, no city below ~100k population (use country if the city is small enough to identify the company).

**Placeholders:** `{EMPLOYEE_FIRST_NAME}`, `{FUNCTION}`, `{CITY}`, `{COUNTRY}`, `{STAGE}`, `{HEADCOUNT}`, `{INDUSTRY}`, `{YEARS}`, `{SKILLS_THEY_CAN_TEACH}`, `{SKILLS_THEY_WANT}`, `{REASONING}`, `{YOUR_NAME}`

---

**Subject line:**
- A possible MENT match — care to meet?
- I think I've found a useful match for you

---

**Body:**

Hi {EMPLOYEE_FIRST_NAME},

I'd like to suggest a match. Here's their anonymized profile:

- **Function:** {FUNCTION}
- **Location:** {CITY}, {COUNTRY}
- **Company:** {STAGE}, around {HEADCOUNT} people, in {INDUSTRY}
- **Years of experience:** {YEARS}
- **Can mentor on:** {SKILLS_THEY_CAN_TEACH}
- **Wants to grow in:** {SKILLS_THEY_WANT}

**Why I think this could work:**

{REASONING — 2-3 sentences. Examples:
"They want to learn growth analytics — exactly what you've shipped at scale. In return, you've mentioned wanting to learn brand storytelling, and they've led the last two years of brand work at their company. Different industries, same underlying problem."
or
"They've spent five years scaling a sales team from three to twelve, which is the exact transition you're heading into. No competitive overlap with you."}

This person is also seeing your anonymized profile and being asked the same question right now. Names and companies are revealed only if you both say yes.

**Are you open to meeting them for a 30-minute conversation?**

Just reply to this email with one of:

- **Yes** — I'll send a full intro (names, companies, calendar link) once they say yes too.
- **Not this one** — no explanation needed; I'll keep matching you with others.
- **Not right now** — I'll pause your matches for two weeks and come back.

— {YOUR_NAME}
